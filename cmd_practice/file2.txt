Ubuntu is a popular open-source operating system based on Linux, known for its stability, security, and user-friendly interface. It is widely used on desktops, laptops, servers, and even cloud platforms. Ubuntu provides thousands of free applications through its Software Center, including tools for programming, office work, multimedia, and internet use. It receives regular updates and long-term support versions, which makes it reliable for both personal and professional use. Because it is open source, users can customize Ubuntu according to their needs, making it a powerful and flexible operating system.
